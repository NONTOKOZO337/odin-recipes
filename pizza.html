Pizza Recipe

Meaty something Pizza with extra Mozzarella Cheese.YUMMY!!!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Bacon
  • Beef
  • Tomato paste
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Add 1 cup of warm water
  2. add a teaspoon of yeast
  3. Add floor in one bowl and combine. Let it sit for an hour
  4. Strecth your dought to a round
  5. Bake in the oven for about 30min
  6. Add tomato paste
  7. Add your meats of your choice and pappers/veggies
  8. bake again for 20min
  9. Add your cheese on top, and bake until cheese melts.
