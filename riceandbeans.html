Rice and beans recipe

Nutritiuos rice and beans dish

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 1 cup of black beans
  • Pasley
  • spices of choice
  • veggies

Steps

  1. Add 3 cups of water in a pot. add rice and bring to a boil.
  2. add boil beans until soft
  3. in another pot, add oil and veggies. cook in mid heat
  4. Add desired spices
  5. add water. cook for 15 min
  6. in a bowl, add dish rice and beans. slightly mix
  7. top with pasley and serve hot.
  8. enjoy!!
